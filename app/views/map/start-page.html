Make a plea for an offence

Use this service to plead guilty or not guilty online.

You must be either:

  • The person charged with the offence
  • an official representative of the company charged with the offece

Before you start

Are you representing yourself or a company?

To use this service you need:

For all pleas
  • the Single Justice Procedure notice sent to you
  • your National Insurance number
  • details of your income, benefits and outgoings
  • your employer's details
  • your email address
Additional information needed for not guilty pleas
  • your witness' name, address, age and date of birth
  • any dates you can't attend court

To use this service you need:

For all pleas:
  • the Single Justice Procedure notice sent to you
  • the company you represents financial information
  • an email address
Additional information needed for not guilty pleas
  • your witness' name, address, age and date of birth
  • any dates you can't attend court
